Biography

Biography

A lifelong student of sound, REBRND is actively discovering the knowledge and power behind music in hopes of creating art that will encourage optimism. As an electronic music producer, DJ, and artist REBRND is immersed in all aspects of music from performances to creating uplifting energetic tracks. His goal is to use his music as a tool to encourage people to become courageous, caring and overall feel better about themselves and the world around them after. Whether it be listening to his music, going to his shows or coming across any of his platforms you will find yourself discovering an energetic sound that carries a good rhythm and even better message.

yesterday was good, today will be better

His artist name “REBRND” (pronounced rebrand) represents a reminder that people can recreate themselves, or in other words rebrand themselves, into better versions of themselves at any point throughout their lifetime. REBRND believes that rebranding or recreating yourself often is the purpose of life and is what makes life worth living. This serves as motivation for him to constantly grow both as an artist and as a person. With this motto REBRND will continue to provide better content and shows to share with the world and is the driving force behind creating positive music in the House music genre.
